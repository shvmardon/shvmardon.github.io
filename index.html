I am an economist and educational consultant currently pursuing a Master's degree in Economics and Policy Evaluation at Sapienza University of Rome. My academic background spans economics, foreign economic activity, and budget control — shaped by studies at the University of World Economy and Diplomacy (UWED) and Tashkent State University of Economics (TSUE).

My work sits at the intersection of macroeconomics, development, and institutional analysis, with a particular interest in how policy design translates into measurable outcomes. I approach questions empirically, drawing on quasi-experimental methods and cross-country data to understand what actually moves the needle.
